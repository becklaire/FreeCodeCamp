Here's a brief time line of Nikola Tesla's key dates:

  • 1859: Nikola Tesla is born during a stormy night.
  • 1881: Tesla begins his carrer as Chief Electrical Engineer in Budapest. A year after, he's working within the Edison Compagny.
  • 1887: 2 years after his first patent, Nikola Tesla came up with an induction motor that was operated by alternating current.
  • 1890: Tesla studies on Wireless Power transmission allows him to create his famous "Tesla coil"
  • 1943: At age 86, Nikola Tesla dies in a hotel Room in New York
